# App Store Metadata

## Promotional Text
Track feeds, sleep, diapers and growth in one tap. The calmest newborn tracker for 3 AM nights.

## Description
Lullab is the simplest newborn tracker designed for exhausted parents. When you are up at 3 AM with a fussy baby, the last thing you need is a complicated app. Lullab lets you log feedings, sleep, diaper changes, and growth with a single tap so you can get back to soothing your little one.

DESIGNED FOR SLEEP-DEPRIVED PARENTS

We know the newborn phase is overwhelming. That is why Lullab is built around one principle: one-handed, one-tap logging. No tiny buttons. No confusing menus. Just open the app, tap, and you are done.

FEEDING TRACKER

- Log breastfeeding sessions with left and right side tracking
- Track bottle feedings with volume in ml or oz
- Record solid food introductions as your baby grows
- See time since last feeding at a glance on the home screen

SLEEP TRACKER

- Start and stop sleep timers with one tap
- Live Activity support so you can see the timer on your Lock Screen
- Track naps and nighttime sleep separately
- View daily and weekly sleep summaries to spot patterns

DIAPER TRACKER

- Quick log for wet, dirty, or mixed diaper changes
- See time since last change right on the home screen
- Track daily diaper counts for pediatrician visits

GROWTH TRACKER

- Record weight, height, and head circumference
- Visual growth charts to monitor your babys progress
- Compare against WHO growth percentile standards

TIMELINE VIEW

- See all events in a clean chronological timeline
- Filter by event type to focus on what matters
- Scroll through days and weeks of history

DARK MODE FIRST

- Designed for those middle-of-the-night sessions
- Soothing dark theme that wont wake your partner
- Gentle colors that are easy on tired eyes

DATA YOU OWN

- All data stored locally on your device
- iCloud sync keeps data safe across devices
- No account required to start tracking
